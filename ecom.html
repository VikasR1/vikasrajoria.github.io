E-commerce Automation Tool

CONCEPT

This is an automation bot, it will be able to scrape the specified product details from an E-commerce site at a scheduled time, and then will send the updated information to its users daily.

About

As you know the prices of products are changing rapidly these days. Sometimes, the prices become too high, and sometimes those products are available at a reasonable rate. And we all want to get the product at a reasonable cost. For that, we usually keep monitoring the prices manually.

Though it is easy to check the price manually for limited products. But when it comes to checking the price of multiple products at the same time. It becomes a challenging task for an individual.

So to do such kind of regular activities, an automation bot can become a helping hand for you. It will go to that site for you and scrap the required details and send those details to you on daily basis at a specified time.
