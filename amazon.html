About

The Internet is a data store of the world's information in many formats. It may be in text, media, data, or in any other format. In the modern world, data is crucial for the success of any business. Unfortunately, most of the websites do not provide to display their data in your local system or to run on personal sites. This is where web scraping tools can help you.

The uses of scraped data for business as well as for personal use are endless. Each business and individuals has their own needs for gathering data.

The data will be useful for following fields but not limited to these

  • Research
  • Machine Learning
  • Marketing
  • Real State
  • E-commerce
  • Travel & Tourism
  • Sports analytics
  • Social Media
